Roles and Responsibilities

  • filter_dramaBusines Integrator / Business Analyst

    The liaison between the business and IT. Aids with demand management, and working with the business on user stories and other project needs.  This role will may also fulfill the Lilly technical role, that will provide overall application architecture guidance and work with other Lilly application teams that require connection configuration.

  • placeProduct (System) Owner

    Owns the solution requirements (user stories) and is representative of all the stakeholders on a project.  Also responsible for clarifying and prioritizing user stories, making decisions where necessary, facilitating communication between implementation team and project stakeholders, and ultimately accepting or rejecting delivered user stories.

  • whatshotSystem Custodian

    Accountable to the Application Product Owner; ensures the application is installed, maintained, supported and validated according to applicable corporate and local policies and procedures.  Responsible for the validation of the system.

  • filter_dramaProject Manager

    Manages the planning process, overall schedule, resource allocation and financials.  They will also be active in project status reporting using Mission Control.

  • filter_dramaTest Lead

    Develops test plans/scripts/strategies, defines and distributes environment to testers, and is responsible for managing end-to-end test execution.

  • filter_dramaFunctional Lead

    Responsible for helping refine and maintain user stories/acceptance criteria in Mission Control.  They will also help facilitate UAT, user/stakeholder education, and aid with testing.

  • filter_dramaTechnical Lead

    Leads the estimation of development efforts for user stories, manages the development team, and runs the Iteration Planning and Review workshops.  Guides developers in the planning poker sessions for estimating user stories.

  • filter_dramaScrum Master

    Schedules and facilitates all Scrum meetings, in addition to  leading the daily "scrum" stand-up meeting, and implementing and ensuring scrum process and principles are followed. Assists with OCM with product owner and business team and drives continuous improvements among the team increasing velocity.

  • filter_dramaDeveloper

    Assist in user story estimation and develops user stories to acceptance criteria specifications.
